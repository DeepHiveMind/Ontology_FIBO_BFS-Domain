Annotations (5)

  • adaptedFrom "OMG Property and Casualty Information Models, dtc/12-01-04, Annex A, Glossary of Data Model Terms and Definitions" (string)
  • label "party in role" ()
  • definition "a relative concept that ties an independent party to a specific role they are standing in in which they play some part i.e. are party to" (string)
  • example "Examples include organization member, issuer, owner, partner in a partnership, shareholder, etc." (string)
  • scopeNote "The concept of a party in a role refers only to those contexts in which in natural English one would call someone a 'party' for example being party to a contract or to a transaction; it does not cover entities as performing some role in some activity or process (the separate concept Actor covers that). A good test is whether the relative thing defined as PartyInRole can be sensibly said to have a part or play a part in something. Corresponds to the English (not data modeling) sense of the word 'Party'." (string)

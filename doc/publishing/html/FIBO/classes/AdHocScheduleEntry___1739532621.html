Annotations (4)

  • usageNote "Other ontologies can extend AdHocScheduleEntry as needed. In particular, the Occurrences ontology extends AdHocScheduleEntry to comprise an OccurrenceKind. The meaning is that an ad hoc schedule entry comprises a date and an event which is scheduled to occur on that date; in other words Occurrence of the OccurrenceKind should happen on the Date of the OccurrenceKind." (string)
  • usageNote "The Date of an AdHocScheduleEntry can be an ExplicitDate or any kind of CalculatedDate, such as: * An OccurrenceBasedDate -- a Date that itself is defined by an Occurrence (see the Occurrences ontology) * A RelativeDate - a Date relative to another Date, such as T+3 * A SpecifiedDate - a Date that is defined by an arbitrary rule" (string)
  • label "ad hoc schedule entry" ()
  • definition "an entry, including a date, among multiple ad hoc schedule entries that jointly make up an AdHocSchedule" (string)

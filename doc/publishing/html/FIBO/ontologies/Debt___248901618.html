  • editorialNote "Note that in the original 'red' version of this ontology, there were conflicting and confusing definitions related to credit and debt whereby accounting definitions were mixed in with instrument-related definitions. The accounting terms, which conflated debt and debit, among other things, have been removed. For example, the concepts of credit and debit may specify balance sheet entries. Definitions for balance sheet credit and balance sheet debit have been eliminated from this ontology, however, as they belong somewhere else in an accounting ontology, in the context of defining a general ledger and balance sheet, rather than herein."

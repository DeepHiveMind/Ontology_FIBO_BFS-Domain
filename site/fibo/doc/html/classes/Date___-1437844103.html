Annotations (3)

  • usageNote "A Date may or may not have a value, and may be explicit or calculated. A Date that has a value is one that is either explicitly set as a literal when it is created, or is some form of CalculatedDate. In an instance of Date, the existence of the 'hasDateValue' property both indicates that the Date is known, and gives the value of the Date. A Date that does not have a value is one that is some form of CalculatedDate, in which the actual date has not (yet) been established." (string)

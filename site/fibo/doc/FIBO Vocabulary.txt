    <h1>
      EDM Council FIBO Vocabulary
    </h1>
    <h2>Products</h2>
    <p>
The FIBO vocabulary is distributed using the Simple Knowledge Organization System (<a href="https://www.w3.org/2004/02/skos/">SKOS</a>). SKOS is a W3C standard.
</p>
FIBO Vocabulary (Development) (<b>download</b> in (<a href="http://spec.edmcouncil.org/fibo/vocabulary/master/latest/fibo-vD.rdf">RDF-XML</a), (<a href="http://spec.edmcouncil.org/fibo/vocabulary/master/latest/fibo-vD.ttl"<Turtle</a>), (<a href="http://spec.edmcouncil.org/fibo/vocabulary/master/latest/fibo-vD.jsonld"<JSON-LD<a/>)
</p>
FIBO Vocabulary (Production) (<b>download</b> in (<a href="http://spec.edmcouncil.org/fibo/vocabulary/master/latest/fibo-vP.rdf">RDF-XML</a), (<a href="http://spec.edmcouncil.org/fibo/vocabulary/master/latest/fibo-vP.ttl"<Turtle</a>), (<a href="http://spec.edmcouncil.org/fibo/vocabulary/master/latest/fibo-vP.jsonld"<JSON-LD<a/>)
</p>
This is a machine-readable file giving terms, definitions and relationships. These are in an extended Simple Knowledge Organization System (SKOS) format, where FIBO relationships are represented as subproperties
of the standard <i>isRelatedTo</i> property, rather than as SKOS Concepts.

The FIBO Vocabulary machine-readable files are intended to provide input to a range of tools, usually characterized as Glossary or Vocabulary tools.
These tools are generally used to provide further business-facing representations of the content of these files.
</p>

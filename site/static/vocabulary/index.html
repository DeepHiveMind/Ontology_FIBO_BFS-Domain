EDM Council FIBO Vocabulary

The FIBO vocabulary is distributed using the Simple Knowledge Organization System (SKOS). It is a machine-readable dictionary of the FIBO terminology. At the moment, the FIBO Vocabulary is only available in Development mode, in a variety of RDF formats.
